Focaccia with caramelized Onions,
Cranberries, and Brie

Wowo your taste bus with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart
cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Ingredients

    For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups(350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions,yhinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes(scrape rind off with a paring knife if desiderd, thought it's not necessary
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or forzen cranberries
